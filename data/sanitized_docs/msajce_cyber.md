MSAJCE — Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Engineering

The B.E. Computer Science and Engineering with Specialization in Cyber Security (CSCS) is an undergraduate engineering degree program focused on equipping students with the knowledge and skills needed to protect computer systems, networks, and data from cyber threats and attacks.

**Objective:**

To develop experts in the field of information security, ethical hacking, and digital forensics.

**Key Features:**

1. Specialized Curriculum — Focused on security principles, network security, cryptography, ethical hacking, and digital forensics. Includes foundational courses in computer science.
2. Hands-on Learning — Labs and simulation environments for penetration testing, secure coding, and threat analysis. Capstone projects and internships.
3. Legal and Ethical Foundations — Cyber laws and ethics, compliance, and governance integrated into the curriculum.
4. Emerging Technologies — Security in AI, IoT, Cloud computing, and Blockchain.

**Top Recruiters:**

1. Government & Defense Agencies
2. IT and Cyber Security Firms
3. Banks and Financial Institutions
4. Healthcare and Insurance Companies
5. E-commerce and Cloud Service Providers

**Programme Details:**

- Duration: 4 years (Regular) / 3 years (Lateral Entry)
- No. of Semesters: 8 (Regular) / 6 (Lateral Entry)
- Intake / No. of Seats: Total - 30 (Government - 15, Management - 15)
- Eligibility: 10+2 system of Education with a pass in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- Curriculum & Syllabus: 2021 Regulation
- Scope for Higher Studies: M.E. / M.Tech. / M.B.A.
