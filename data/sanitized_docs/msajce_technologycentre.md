Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) is committed to equipping students with skills aligned with emerging industrial demands. Established in the year 2019-2020, various Technology Centres offer education, training, and upskilling in niche technologies. These courses are designed based on the recommendations of AICTE and the India Skill Report 2020, featuring cutting-edge facilities that foster an ambient learning environment for research and innovation. Each centre collaborates with industry and government organizations to provide certifications, onsite and offsite projects, and internships.

**Robotics Process Automation (RPA) with Automation Anywhere**  
MSAJCE has partnered with Automation Anywhere to establish the AAU powered Bot Lab, which focuses on creating software robots that automate business processes. Students will gain knowledge about the Automation Anywhere Enterprise Platform, how to create bots, and apply methodologies to solve real-world problems. Successful participants will receive globally recognized certifications.

**Course Offered:**  
- Robotic Process Automation  
- Certifications:  
   - Automation Anywhere Certified Essential RPA Professional  
   - Automation Anywhere Certified Advanced RPA Professional  
   - Automation Anywhere Certified Master RPA Professional  

**CISCO Networking Academy Centre**  
MSAJCE collaborates with CISCO to establish the Networking Academy, providing students with hands-on learning in networking, security, and cloud technologies. The program prepares students for networking jobs worldwide.

**Courses Offered:**  
- Network Essentials  
- Mobility Fundamentals  
- CCNA: Introduction to Networks  
- CCNA: Switching, Routing, and Wireless Essentials  
- CCNA: Enterprise Networking, Security and Automation  

**Certifications:**  
- CISCO Certified Network Associate (CCNA)  
- Implementing CISCO Enterprise Network Core Technologies  

**Centre for 3D Printing and Additive Manufacturing**  
Students will gain knowledge in 3D printing technologies, CAD modeling, and various processes for digital manufacturing. Practical sessions will enhance their skills in creating products through additive manufacturing.

**Centre for Amazon Web Services (AWS) & Cloud Computing**  
Objectives include understanding fundamentals of building IT infrastructure on AWS, configuring various cloud services, and creating highly available applications. 

**Cyber Security Academy with Palo Alto Systems**  
Students will learn general security concepts, the fundamentals of cybersecurity, and strategies for network defense. They will develop skills to identify and mitigate attacks against enterprise networks.

**Embedded & IoT Centre**  
Students will learn IoT architectures, protocols, and how to build simple IoT systems using Arduino and Raspberry Pi.
