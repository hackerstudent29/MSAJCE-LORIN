MSAJCE Placement Cell Overview: In a competitive job market, employers expect graduates to have a range of skills and right experience of the workplace. MSAJCE provides utmost care, support, and ample opportunities to help students develop requisite skills and expertise and make them employable graduates.

**Placement Cell Objectives:**

1. To provide Employment Opportunities for students in reputed companies.
2. To organize On and Off Campus Recruitment Drives for students.
3. To Train students on employability skills such as Aptitude Skills, Communication Skills, and Technical Skills.
4. To conduct Value Added Certification courses to gain domain-specific knowledge.
5. To conduct Mock Aptitude, Mock Interview, Mock GD to prepare students to face interviews with confidence.
6. To arrange In-plant training, Internship, and Industrial visits.
7. To organize workshops on Recent Technologies by industrial experts which benefit students to understand technological innovations.

Besides providing support to entrepreneurial ventures, MSAJCE keeps its employability services up to date with the demands of the job market and aims to ensure that graduates are in the strongest position possible when they leave.

**MSAJCE Placement Facilities:**

1. Well-furnished air-conditioned rooms for written tests, group discussions, and face-to-face interviews.
2. Well-established Computer labs for online training and examinations.
3. Air-conditioned Auditorium / Seminar Halls / Meeting Halls.
4. Dedicated AC rooms for conducting group discussions.
5. Career counselling is available on campus directing students to choose the best possible stream that meets their requirement. The students have mentors who handle their problems and help them focus on their targets.
