MSAJCE — Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Engineering

The role of Information Technology is vital in every aspect of human life. Information technology is the study and use of systems for storing, retrieving, and processing information. With the introduction of computers, the business world has changed forever; using computers ensures smooth running of the business. Information technology helps the business world to view changes in global markets faster than they usually do. Due to the development of this technology, globalization has increased, and the world is brought closer. Information is stored and shared quickly and easily across the globe. Communication has become easier and cheaper. Using the internet and Skype, people can speak to each other all over the globe. The key fields include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Networking, Mobile Computing, Blockchain, and more, which have tremendous job openings.
