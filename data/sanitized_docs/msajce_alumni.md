**Alumni Association Overview and Objectives**

Alumni association of Mohamed Sathak AJ College of Engineering reconnects and fosters intellectual and emotional relationships between alumni and the parent organization. It aims to bring goodwill and voluntary commitment to support future engineers and provide industry-institute relationships while participating in various activities organized by the college.

**Objectives:**
1. To foster a sense of connectedness back to the institution
2. To establish and renew student relationships
3. To involve in various activities of the college
4. To provide assistance to needful students by way of funds and scholarships
5. To gather and maintain a database of employment and assist in placements
6. To arrange teaching and training classes for students
7. To contribute to the overall upgrading of the institution
