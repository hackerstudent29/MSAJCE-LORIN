The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is a methodology adopted by the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, to rank institutions of higher education across the country. MSAJCE participates in NIRF rankings annually and submits data across three categories: Engineering, Overall, and Innovation.
