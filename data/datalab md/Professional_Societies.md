## 1. CSI — Computer Society of India

### Vision: "IT for Masses"

Keeping in mind the interest of IT professionals and computer users, CSI works towards making the profession an area of choice amongst all sections of society. CSI regularly organizes conferences, conventions, lectures, projects, and awards. It also provides training for physically challenged citizens ('Punarjani') and assists governments in formulating IT strategy and planning.

### Region VII Covers:

Tamil Nadu, Pondicherry, Andaman and Nicobar, Kerala, Lakshadweep.

### Kanchipuram Chapter

The Society has developed a well-established network across the country. Activities for students include lecture meetings, seminars, conferences, training programs, programming contests, and industrial visits. CSI's Educational Directorate undertakes certification of professionals on the latest technologies and offers distance education in Business Domain areas.

## 2. IEEE — Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers

The IEEE is a United States-based non-profit organization and the world's leading professional association for the advancement of technology. It covers areas ranging from aerospace systems, electrical, electronics, computers, telecommunications, biomedical engineering, electric power, and consumer electronics. IEEE publishes technical literature and highly qualified research articles across all engineering domains.

### Student Branch Activities:

- Participation in regional conferences, workshops and competitions
- Development of leadership, interpersonal and team building skills
- Scholarships and project/design programs and student paper contests
- Establishment of a Student Branch Library with IEEE publications
- Access to IEEE online services and resources; establish a website
- Organize various awareness programs for school students and public

## 3. IETE — Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers

IETE is one of the leading Professional Societies in India for Electronics and Communication Engineers. MSAJCE established its IETE Students Forum on 16/08/2017 with 100 active members. The forum organizes guest lectures, seminars, workshops, and conferences. Student members benefit from technical visits, practical training, and project work in R&D; laboratories and industries.

## 5. ISHRAE — Indian Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air Conditioning Engineers

ISHRAE was founded in 1981 at New Delhi by a group of eminent HVAC&R; professionals. It operates from 41 chapters and sub-chapters spread all over India, with HQ in Delhi. ISHRAE works with various Govt. Ministries/Departments including BIS, BEE (ECBC), MoEFCC (Ozone Cell), and NCCD (Ministry of Agriculture). It is looked upon as a repository of technical knowledge in the HVAC&R; and Building Industry field by peer organizations and the Government of India.

### Focus Areas:

- Sustainability
- Green Buildings
- Energy Efficiency
- Environmental Responsibility
- Indoor Air Quality
- Fire & Safety

### Student Branch Activities:

- Excellent networking opportunities to interact with professionals, industry leaders, and key decision makers
- Forum for professional development and continuous training on fundamentals and latest technologies
- Easy access to all ISHRAE Technical Publications, Software, Workshops, Conferences and Training Programs
- Opportunity to work in Chapter Working Committee and Headquarter activities
- Platform for effective marketing and launching of new products through exhibitions and advertisements
- International exposure through International Associates
