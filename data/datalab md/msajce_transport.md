## 1. College Bus Facility — Overview & General Information

Chunk ID: msajce\_page\_04\_chunk\_01 | URL: <https://www.msjace-edu.in/transport.php>

The College is easily accessible from Chennai Central Railway Station, Tambaram Railway Station and CMDA Bus Station by the state operated transport service. In addition, the College provides its own transportation for students and staff residing in and around the campus.

| Fleet     | Count |
|-----------|-------|
| Buses     | 22    |
| Tata ACE  | 1     |
| Ambulance | 1     |

**Service Area:** Chennai, Chengalpattu, Kanchipuram and Thiruvallur districts — available on all working days.

### **Uses of Bus Facility:**

- Daily commute for students and staff
- Local social service activities
- Sports activities
- NCC / NSS activities
- Placement and Training activities
- Industrial visits
- Educational trips

**Transport Committee:** A committee headed by a Convener manages smooth bus operations. One teaching and one supporting staff are in-charge of each bus. The committee meets once every six months; the Principal attends the meeting.
