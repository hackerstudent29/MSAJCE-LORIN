B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) is a specialized undergraduate engineering program designed to blend core computer science education with insights into business systems and management. Introduced in collaboration with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) to bridge the gap between traditional computer science education and the dynamic needs of the IT and business world in the era of Business 4.0.

Curriculum covers: Analytics, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Business Analytics, Financial Management, Economics, Internet of Things, Design Thinking, humanities and value-added science.

### Key Features:

- Balanced Curriculum — Core CS (Data Structures, Algorithms, OS, DBMS, AI/ML) + Business subjects (Economics, Financial Management, Business Process Management).
- Industry Collaboration — Designed in partnership with TCS; emphasis on employability and industry readiness.
- Focus Areas — Problem-solving using computational thinking, Business analytics, IT systems for business applications.
- Soft Skills Training — Communication, Teamwork, Entrepreneurial thinking.

Benefits Over Regular CSE: More interdisciplinary exposure; better suited for tech-enabled business functions; greater alignment with industry expectations in IT services and consulting.

#### Vision

To be a centre of excellence for transforming students into proficient IT Professionals with Business Skills through sustainable practices.

#### Mission

- M1. Impart core fundamental knowledge and necessary skills in Information Science and Business through innovative teaching and learning methodology.
