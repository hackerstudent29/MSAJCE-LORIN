# MSAJCE — Anti-Ragging Policy and Detailed Punishments

Source: AICTE/UGC Regulatory Norms & MSAJCE Statutory Body | Section: Student Conduct

---

## 1. Zero Tolerance Policy
Mohamed Sathak A.J. College of Engineering (MSAJCE) maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy towards ragging. Ragging in any form is totally prohibited both inside and outside the campus. The Anti-Ragging activities are governed by the Principal, supported by dedicated Anti-Ragging Committees and Squads.

## 2. Comprehensive List of Punishments
Depending on the nature and gravity of the guilt established by the Anti-Ragging Squad, those found guilty may be awarded one or more of the following punishments:

| Level | Punishment Type | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Academic** | Suspension | Suspension from attending classes and academic privileges for a specified period. |
| **Financial** | Withholding Benefits | Withholding or withdrawing scholarships, fellowships, and other financial benefits. |
| **Evaluation** | Debarring from Exams | Debarring from appearing in any test, examination, or other evaluation process. |
| **Results** | Withholding Results | Official results may be withheld by the institution. |
| **Representation** | Extracurricular Ban | Debarring from representing the institution in any regional, national, or international meet, tournament, youth festival, etc. |
| **Residential** | Hostel Expulsion | Immediate suspension or permanent expulsion from the college hostel. |
| **Administrative** | Admission Cancellation | Complete cancellation of the student's admission to the college. |
| **Legal** | FIR Filing | Filing of a First Information Report (FIR) within 24 hours with local police and authorities. |
| **Rustication** | Semester Ban | Rustication from the institution for a period ranging from 1 to 4 semesters. |
| **Expulsion** | Permanent Ban | Permanent expulsion from the institution and consequent debarring from admission to any other institution for a specified period. |

## 3. Collective Punishment
When the persons committing or abetting the act of ragging are not identified, the institution will resort to **Collective Punishment** as a deterrent measure to ensure a ragging-free campus.

## 4. Appellate Authority
As per UGC Regulations, an appeal against the order of punishment by the Anti-Ragging Committee of MSAJCE lies with the **Vice-Chancellor of Anna University, Chennai**.
