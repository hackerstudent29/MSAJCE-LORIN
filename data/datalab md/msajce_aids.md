Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science — MSAJCE

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the process of creating machines to think like humans. AI is a term used to describe the simulation of human intelligence processes in machines. It typically involves sophisticated algorithms that enable computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition and decision making.

Data Science is a field of knowledge which applies advanced statistical and mathematical techniques to large datasets in order to extract knowledge from them. Data can be structured like a spreadsheet or unstructured like text or video data. Data science is the application of techniques from statistics, mathematics, computer programming and other fields to solve real-world problems using information technology.

Due to the exploration of the Internet, AI and Data Science deliver data-driven solutions using computational principles, methods, and systems for extracting information from data and modern computational systems that reveal capabilities of insight, way of thinking, learning, and action that are typical of human intelligence.

B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AIDS) has emerged as one of the most high-growth, dynamic, and lucrative careers in technology since 2015. This course aims at providing core technologies such as artificial intelligence, data mining, and data modeling, and also gives intensive inputs in areas of machine learning and big data analytics. Students gain cross-disciplinary skills across fields such as statistics, computer science, machine learning, and logic. Career opportunities exist in healthcare, business, e-commerce, social networking companies, climatology, biotechnology, genetics, and other important areas. The major focus of this programme is to equip students with statistical, mathematical reasoning, machine learning, knowledge discovery, and visualization skills.

The programme also deals with core principles and ethical issues in Data Science and AI, and motivates students to explore cutting-edge applications related to Big Data, Neural Networks, and Deep Learning. Applications like Alexa/Siri, Erica/Sophia, Tesla's driverless car, Chat-bot, etc. are becoming common. AI-powered Robots can take over human life-endangering jobs like firefighting, deep-sea oil drilling, and mining works with high mortality rates.

## 3. AI and Data Science Growth in India 2025–2030 — Key Drivers and Job Roles

#### AI and Data Science Growth in India by 2025–2030

The Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science sectors in India are expected to have explosive growth over the next five years. AI-driven solutions and data analytics are driving better decisions across all sectors, and the demand for professionals in these fields is expected to grow significantly.

#### Key Drivers:

- Increased AI Adoption Across Industries — Healthcare, Finance, E-commerce, Telecommunications, Automotive, and Manufacturing. AI will be used for Predictive analytics, Personalized services, Data-driven decision-making, and Automation. AI-driven solutions in core business functions (HR, finance, customer service).
- Government Support and Initiatives — Digital India, National AI Strategy, and the National AI Mission initiatives boost AI research, startups, and adoption across public services. India aims to be a global leader in AI for Smart cities, Healthcare, and Agriculture.
- Rising Demand for Data-Driven Decision Making — Customer segmentation, Fraud detection, and Supply chain optimization.
- Growth in AI-Powered Startups — AI startups expected to double in the next few years, creating job opportunities for AI engineers, data scientists, and product managers.
- Integration of AI into Business Functions — Finance, retail, e-commerce, and manufacturing increasingly adopting AI-based automation to streamline operations, reduce costs, and offer personalized experiences.

#### Key AI and Data Science Job Roles in India (2025–2030):

- Data Scientist

Roles: Analyze large datasets to derive actionable insights, build predictive models, help organizations make data-driven decisions.

Skills: Python, R, SQL, Machine Learning Algorithms, Statistical analysis, Data visualization (Tableau, Power BI), Deep learning.

Demand: High demand across healthcare, finance, retail, and e-commerce.

- Machine Learning Engineer

Roles: Build and deploy machine learning models, implement algorithms, optimize model performance for real-world applications.

Skills: Python, TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch, Neural networks, Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), Algorithm optimization.

Demand: Rising, especially in startups and tech giants as companies adopt more AI solutions.

- AI Research Scientist

Roles: Conduct cutting-edge research in AI; focus on Deep learning, Reinforcement learning, Computer vision, and NLP.

Skills: Machine learning algorithms, Python, Mathematical modeling, Research publications, Deep learning frameworks.

Demand: High demand due to growing AI investment from global tech companies and research labs.

- Data Analyst

Roles: Collect, process, and analyze data to uncover trends, create dashboards, and support decision-making with data-driven insights.

Skills: Excel, SQL, Python, Tableau, Power BI, R, SAS.

Demand: Increasing in finance, retail, and healthcare as companies focus on data-driven decision-making.

- Data Engineer

Roles: Design, construct, and maintain large-scale data pipelines; work with big data technologies to ensure data is stored, processed, and accessible.

Skills: SQL, Python, Hadoop, Spark, Kafka, AWS, Google Cloud, ETL processes.

Demand: Rising with the growth of big data and cloud computing in data-driven companies.

- AI Product Manager

Roles: Oversee the development and deployment of AI-powered products; manage cross-functional teams of engineers and data scientists.

Skills: Product lifecycle management, Agile methodology, Machine learning, Project management, Business strategy.

Demand: Rising with AI product development across industries.

- Business Intelligence (BI) Developer

Roles: Design and develop BI tools to collect, store, and analyze business data; help businesses make strategic decisions using AI-driven insights.

Skills: SQL, Power BI, Tableau, data visualization, reporting tools, Python.

Demand: Increasing as companies shift to data-driven strategies, particularly in e-commerce and retail.

- Natural Language Processing (NLP) Engineer

Roles: Work on algorithms and models that allow machines to process and understand human language, like chatbots, virtual assistants, and sentiment analysis.

Skills: Python, NLP frameworks (NLTK, SpaCy, Hugging Face), Machine learning, Deep learning, Text mining.

Demand: Growing in e-commerce, finance, and telecommunications with the rise of AI chatbots, voice assistants, and text analytics.
