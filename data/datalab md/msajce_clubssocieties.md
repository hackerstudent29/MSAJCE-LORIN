Context: Describes the MSAJCE Science Club — its purpose of inviting scientists, three sections (Physics, Chemistry, General), guiding principles, and planned activities including experiments and workshops.

MSAJCE Science Club

#### **Introduction:**

The Science Club was started with an aim to invite eminent scientists who would share their experience with students and motivate them to gain scientific knowledge. Activities are designed primarily to introduce students to undertake interesting and exciting science and technology projects. The Science Club consists of a group of students who want to explore the many branches of engineering, mathematics, and science. The club is open to all students.

The purpose of the Science Club is to promote interest, understanding, and knowledge of the scientific world amongst college students and the local community. Science Club is one of the largest clubs in the college. The club is divided into three sections: Physics, Chemistry, and General.

The club aims to arouse engineering students' interest in learning science, promoting academic achievement in Science subjects. It also helps students to learn more about science outside the classroom and teaches how to apply scientific knowledge in daily life through activities. The club also guides students to develop an independent, logical and creative mind.

Activities include experiments in: making electronic components, making invisible ink, electroplating, biodiesels, fire writing, and making venation of leaves. Information about the science club and day-to-day updates are displayed on the notice board.

#### **Objectives of Science Club:**

The five Guiding Principles govern how activities have been developed. Investigator activity offers students the opportunity to:

1. Solve a relevant, science-based problem set within a scenario.
2. Work in pairs or small groups or independently.
3. Take part in practical, hands-on science activities.
4. Think and talk about science during the activity and when sharing results.
5. Share results using a variety of media.

#### **Things to Do in Science Club:**

- Conduct Seminars, Workshops, Exhibitions and Quiz competitions to inculcate knowledge in the field of science.
- Make students acquire knowledge by giving lectures once a month.
- Promote the use of IT in learning science.
- Encourage student participation in activities organized by the Science Club.
- Help students promote discussion on recent innovations in the field of science once a month.
- Decorate the walls of the classroom, library and laboratory with scientific pictures and charts prepared by students themselves.

MSAJCE Tamil Mandram (Tamil Language and Culture Club)

#### Introduction:

The Tamil Mandram was formed to bring about a wonderful transformation in the youth to keep the Tamil language alive and thriving. The motto of the Mandram is:

"Thithikkum then tamil mozhi endrum vaazha vendum! Engum vella vendum!"

(Translation: "The sweet honey Tamil language must live forever! Must win everywhere!")

The Mandram works to revive and reconstruct the ancient Tamil culture and the identities left behind by ancestors. The vision is for the youth to take pride in their language and move from just speaking Tamil to actively bringing glory to Tamil through action.

"Vaazha Tamil! Velga Tamil!"

(Translation: "Long live Tamil! Victory to Tamil!")

#### Objectives of Tamil Mandram:

1. To develop Tamil enthusiasm and knowledge among students — this is the primary objective of the Tamil Mandram.
2. To spread ancient Tamil culture and heritage among today's youth.
3. To develop technical knowledge in Tamil.
4. To continuously conduct Tamil events and social activities suited to the times.

#### Events Conducted by Tamil Mandram:

1. Irumugam Oru Agam (Two faces, one heart — elocution/drama)
2. Pessum Padam (Speaking Picture — storytelling)
3. Aadu Puli (Tiger and Goat — traditional game)
4. Kaivanna Kaviyam (Colourful Poetry — art and poetry)
5. Tent Kottai (Tent Camp — cultural event)
6. Vidugadhai Vilaiyattu (Riddle Games)

7. Pirasangatin Thalaiwan (King of Speeches — debate/oratory competition)

MSAJCE Energy and Eco Club

#### Energy Club:

The goal of the Energy Club is to raise and spread knowledge about responsible and sustainable energy use. Given the limited quantity of fossil fuels available, resource management and conservation are urgently needed. Students are introduced to the concept of energy conservation through the Energy Club, as well as the necessity for it and what part they can play in guaranteeing conservation. In order to combat the ongoing energy issue, it helps the students to conserve energy. These actions would support not only the dissemination of information about energy efficiency but also its application in homes and institutions.

#### Eco Club:

The Eco Club holds that nature imparts life lessons in its own steady ways and empowers our young students to share their responsibility for upholding environmental health and taking action to prevent environmental degradation both individually and collectively. The club encourages students to take both individual and collective action to protect the environment.
