Welcome to the Learning Centre — MSAJCE

Library is the Heart of every academic Institution. Our institution encourages all students and staff members to make the best use of the library that has been carefully built up since the inception of the college as a cardinal support service of the Institute. The prime objective of our library is to provide the right information to the right readers at the right time in the right manner by bibliographic sources and full text sources in the form of print as well as digital in the disciplines of Science, Engineering, Technology, Humanities, Management, General and etc.

The collections are kept in separate places such as the stack section, reference section, and Periodical Section to support the scholarly and informational needs of the Institute Community. The library is housed in a spacious area of 8978 Sq.ft (Ground Floor and 1st Floor), fully computerized with more than 29853 books and 1885 reference volumes stacked for the benefit of staff and students. The library subscribes around 35 nos specialized Printed journals and 20 nos magazines. We are also a member of DELNET. J-Gate database is subscribed yearly.

Technology: Bar-coded Technology, open source software called Koha, internet connectivity and WIFI facility are all available.

## 6. Institution Membership and Library Rules and Regulations

Chunk ID: msajce\_library\_chunk\_06 | URL: <https://www.msajce-edu.in/library.php> | ~510 tokens

MSAJCE library institutional memberships (DELNET and J-Gate), and the complete set of library rules, regulations, and fine structure for overdue books.

Institution Membership:

1. Developing Library Network (DELNET) — International Library Network: Avail any book, Xerox or soft copy of any journal article through ILL (Inter Library Loan). URL: <https://delnet.in/>
2. J-Gate (Database): More than 50,684 journals are available in the online website. URL: <https://jgateplus.com/>

Library Rules and Regulations:

- All Staff and Students are the members of our College Library.
- All students are eligible to borrow 18 Books.
- All staff are eligible to borrow Books.
- Reference books and periodicals will NOT be issued.
- Library ID is not transferable.
- Member should notify the Librarian any loss of cards and change of address immediately.
- The User may reserve a book in circulation. He will be notified as soon as the reserved book is returned. The book will be retained at the circulation desk for one day from the date of return.
- Books will be issued for a short duration of 30 days only.

Fine Structure for Overdue Books:

- After 30 days — First 7 days (days 31–37): Rs. 1.00 per day per book
- After 30 days — 8 to 14 days (days 38–44): Rs. 2.00 per day per book
- After 30 days — 15 days onwards (day 45+): Rs. 5.00 per day per book

Additional Rules:

- Silence should be observed in the Library.
- Users should leave personal belongings (bags, briefcase, notebooks, files) at the entrance.
- Loss of books must be reported immediately to the Librarian. If the borrower is unable to replace the lost book, the cost (double amount) will be recovered from the borrower.
- All books must be returned to the Library in time for physical stock verification.
- Students are advised to check whether the book contains all pages intact before borrowing. At the time of returning, if any damage or defect is found, the borrower will be held responsible.

### Library Fine Structure for Overdue Books

| Overdue Period                                            | Fine Rate                 |
|-----------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------|
| Days 31–37 (First 7 days after 30-day loan period)        | Rs. 1.00 per day per book |
| Days 38–44 (8 to 14 days after 30-day loan period)        | Rs. 2.00 per day per book |
| Day 45 onwards (15 days onwards after 30-day loan period) | Rs. 5.00 per day per book |
