MSAJCE Placement Cell Overview: In a competitive job market, employers expect graduates to have a range of skills and right experience of the workplace. MSAJCE provides at most care, support and ample opportunities to help students develop requisite skills and expertise and make them employable graduates. Placement Cell Objectives: (1) To provide Employment Opportunities for students in reputed companies. (2) To organize On and Off Campus Recruitment Drives for students. (3) To Train students on employability skills such as Aptitude Skills, Communication Skills and Technical Skills. (4) To conduct Value Added Certification courses to gain domain specific knowledge. (5) To conduct Mock Aptitude, Mock Interview, Mock GD to prepare students to face the interview with confidence. (6) To arrange In-plant training, Internship and Industrial visits. (7) To organize workshops on Recent Technologies by industrial experts which benefits students to understand technological innovations. Besides providing support to entrepreneurial ventures, MSAJCE keeps its employability services up to date with the demands of the job market and aims to ensure that graduates are in the strongest position possible when they leave.
